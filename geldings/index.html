Cavitate (aka ‘Boomer’)
2008, bay, 16.3h, Thoroughbred gelding.
Not your ordinary OTTB! Boomer was bred and born in his owner’s backyard. She is not a racehorse breeder, but bred this horse out of love for his mother, a Secretariat great-granddaughter, who she owned and rode for years. Boomer had a successful racing career and he retired in the summer of 2013.






Lyndon Acres purchased Boomer in March of 2014!  Look for him out and about!

Just Jack

Laid to rest November 25, 2011
It is with great sadness that we share the loss of Jack.  An off-the-track Thoroughbred, he was an eventing mount for several people before we gave him a home and he excelled as our school horse.  He lived his last two years with a difficult to diagnose condition with dignity and no known discomfort.  Ultimately called Acute Lymphoma, we were glad he was comfortable to the end.  He will be greatly missed by all who knew him.  RIP, loyal friend.
