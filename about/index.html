About the Trainer 
Lynda Lewis is the instructor, trainer and along with her husband Don, the owner of Lyndon Acres. She is a USEA ICP Certified Instructor Level 1 Training and is also an ARIA certified instructor in both Eventing as well as Dressage, safety is always paramount at Lyndon Acres. Lynda has been riding since she was a child in Ohio. There she showed Junior Working Hunters on the ‘A’ Hunter Circuit back when hunters jumped in a polo field NOT an arena!

Lynda has been instructing and training in Texas for nearly 30 years and specializes in horses and/or riders who are new to Eventing and/or Dressage, though she is equally adept at helping those already familiar with the sports to improve their skills and meet their goals! Always striving to better herself as a horsewoman and rider, Lynda clinics with nationally and internationally known clinicians, some of which include: Sally O’Connor, Mary D’Arcy, Bill Woods, Hans Biss, J. Alex Wortmann, Robin Walker and Kathleen Zins to name a few.

Riders and their horses will find themselves learning and improving their horsemanship and riding skills at their own pace with Lynda who is a sympathetic and patient teacher. Both students and horses will feel challenged yet not over-faced. Whether age 7 or 97, Lynda has the compassion and knowledge to adapt to your personal needs as well as the individual needs of any breed of horse.

Facility
Lyndon Acres is a quiet 8 acre facility located conveniently off I35W in Burleson since 1991. Amenities include a lighted arena that has a 20 x 40 sand dressage ring as well as Stadium jumps in it.

There is a four acre pasture for galloping plus a few inviting cross country jumps including a bank, logs and a trakehner. Though we do not offer boarding at this time, you and your horse will receive sympathetic, custom care whether in a lesson or training program!
